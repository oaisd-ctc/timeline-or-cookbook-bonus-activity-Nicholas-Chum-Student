Fried Rice Recipe

Fried Rice Creation Step-by-step

  1. Prepare your rice (1 day old rice), vegetables (peas, corn, etc.), 4-6 eggs, and chicken (cubed chicken breast)
  2. Begin cooking by adding some oil and then cooking the chicken first
  3. Cook chicken until the outside is cooked and then set aside
  4. Cook eggs until scrambled and then set aside
  5. Cook the vegetables until soft and then set aside
  6. Pour the rice in, cook until extremely slightly brown, then begin adding vegetables, eggs, and chicken
  7. Cook for a while while constantly even to ensure that all the rice is evenly cooked
  8. Add sauces such as soy sauce to the side of the pot so it flows in and covers it well
  9. Add salt, sugar, and other spices that you might desire
  10. Cook for a while longer and when done, plate and enjoy!
