Green Curry Recipe

Green Curry Creation Step-by-step

  1. Toast white peppercorn, corriander seeds, and cumin seed until fragrent
  2. Pour your freshly toasted spices into a mortar and pestle and begin to grind and stir the seeds until they become a fine powder (cover with hand if needed)
  3. Keep them into a different plate and then chop up some green peppers and put them into the mortar
  4. Also put shallots/garlic, galangal, kafir lime leaf, coriander root, and lemon grass into mortar
  5. Continue to mash them together and when almost done, add shrimp paste and then continue
  6. Now to the pot, add oil and then your store-bought coconut oil (3 large spoonfuls) and stir a little
  7. Then add the green curry paste and chicken thighs (not chicken breasts) and fish sauce and then stir some more
  8. Add thai eggplants, thai basil, red chilis, bamboo shoots, and kafir lime leaves (all cleaned) and then stir some more
  9. If the curry is too dry, add chicken stock (not water cause water has no flavor) and then stir some more
  10. Add brown sugar and then stir some more
  11. When almost done taste and make sure it's perfect, if needed, add more fish sauce or other ingredients
  12. To finish it up, add MSG to increase the flavor and umami of the green curry
  13. Turn off the heat and serve hot and enjoy!
