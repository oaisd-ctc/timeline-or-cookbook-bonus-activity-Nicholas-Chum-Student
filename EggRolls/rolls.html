Egg Rolls Recipe

Egg Roll Creation Step-by-step

  1. Prepare the filling by adding ground beef, shredded carrots, and other meats and vegetables into the bowl
  2. Stir and add any spices that you would want as well
  3. When ready, also prepare some egg yolk to use it to seal the egg roll
  4. Prepare by taking your egg roll wrappers and unwrapping the wrapper off (keep them together though and use TYJ Spring Roll Pastry)
  5. Take one at a time, lay it out, and put 2-3 inches of filling on one corner horizontally
  6. Roll the filling along with the corner to the half way point, at which you should fold the two horizontal corners in
  7. Continue rolling until there is about 1-2 inches left at the end
  8. At which, put some egg wash on it and close it up
  9. Finally when all of them are made, fry and enjoy with any sauce you want!
