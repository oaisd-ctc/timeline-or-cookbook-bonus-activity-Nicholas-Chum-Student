Pho Noodle Recipe

Pho Noodle Creation Step-by-step

  1. Prepare sliced meats, seafood, and vegetables
  2. Begin boiling a pot of water
  3. In one pot, prepare your broth, adding whatever you want such as bones, your own seasoning, and other delicious ingredients - if you add vegetables, only add solid, large ones (nothing small like mint)
  4. Leave that to cook for 30 mins to an hour or until boiling
  5. Prepare a second pot of boiling water
  6. When the second pot is boiling, take your rice noodles and plunge them in with a strainer to carry them
  7. After about 15 to 20 seconds, take them out and put them in your bowl
  8. Place your thin vegetables and meats into the bowl with your noodles
  9. Bring your bowl to your soup broth and proceed to introduce it to your noodles
  10. Let it soak into your noodles giving it flavor, and cook the other meats and vegetables
  11. When done, turn off the fire on the soup and you can dump the starchy water away
  12. For now, add ingredients such as hoisen sauce, siracha, soy sauce, and other delicious spices into your pho
  13. Enjoy and make sure to clean up afterwards!
